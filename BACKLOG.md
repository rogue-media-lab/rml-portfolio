# Rogue Media Lab — Product Backlog

## Personal Assistant App (Layer 3 — Future)

**Status:** Backlog — do NOT build until Layer 1 (web services) is generating recurring revenue
**Added:** May 12, 2026

### The Problem
- Hiring is broken. Resumes are filtered by ATS. LinkedIn is a static digital resume.
- Everyone has a phone and apps, but nobody has THEIR OWN app.
- Portfolios show what you DID. Resumes list what you KNOW. Neither proves what you CAN DO.

### The Concept
A personal app that replaces resume + portfolio + LinkedIn + daily productivity tools.

Not a resume app. A "second brain" app:

- **Public-facing:** Skills, projects with live demos, work history (replaces resume + LinkedIn)
- **Private-facing:** AI assistant trained on YOUR expertise, task management, notes, goals (replaces Notion/Obsidian)
- **Daily utility:** Actually useful to the owner every day — not just a showcase

### The Pitch
"Everyone has a phone. Everyone has apps. Why doesn't everyone have THEIR OWN app? Not Instagram's app. Not LinkedIn's app. YOUR app. That YOU own. That works FOR you."

### Why It's Layer 3
- It's a product, not a service (different business model than web services)
- Needs a platform to sell on (app store, web marketplace)
- Needs the credibility of Layer 1 (20+ client sites) and Layer 2 (Carus/platform) behind it
- Better story: "I built 20 businesses their websites, then built a tool for myself, now I'm selling it"

### What Already Exists (Prototype)
- Rocky AI assistant — fun chatbot on portfolio (being rebranded to Wayland)
- Wayland — AI that knows the Lab, powers the Lab page experience
- Portfolio site — showcases work and skills
- These ARE the prototype. Build the business first, productize later.

### Potential Features
- AI chatbot trained on owner's expertise
- Project showcase with live demos
- Skills visualization
- Daily task/goal tracking
- Notes and knowledge base
- Public shareable page (replaces LinkedIn profile)
- Private dashboard (replaces Notion)
